Winam Door of Liberty CBO (WINDO-L)

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Winam Door of Liberty aims to empower young youth, Adolescents, and the vulnerable within the community through (economic, social, health, environment, agricultural, education) We identify with communities of need at the household level or groups, whereby we undertake a comprehensive community diagnosis. We work with other partners, collaborators. The organization motto is to embrace persons of all background, regardless of ethnicity, gender, ideology or religious affiliation.
Creating Equality and Equity in Unequal society.
“A society where all persons lives a dignified life with an equal opportunity. Health, Education, Employment regardless of creed, religion”.

What We Do?

Windo-L collaborates with communities to enhance economic prosperity, healthcare, youth employment, education, and agriculture. They prioritize gender equality, affirming community engagement as vital. Initiatives empower women, youth, and vulnerable individuals through healthcare, career guidance, agricultural programs, and entrepreneurial skill development. Our, holistic approach aims to address unemployment, promote self-sufficiency, and foster community-driven solutions

Agriculture

In agriculture, Windo-L promotes sustainable farming practices, empowering communities with agricultural training and resources. We facilitate access to modern techniques, eco-friendly farming, and animal husbandry. Through community engagement, we bolster local agriculture, ensuring food security, economic growth, and environmental preservation. Windo-L works towards a self-sufficient, environmentally conscious agricultural community.

Health Care

Windo-L plays a vital role in promoting community health and well-being. Windo-L collaborates with local healthcare institutions to facilitate access to essential medical services for community members. We raise health awareness through educational programs, emphasizing preventive measures, healthy practices, and mental well-being. Additionally, we support home-based care initiatives for individuals with chronic illnesses. Through partnerships and community engagement, Windo-L fosters a supportive environment, advocating for health-promoting practices and ensuring access to available healthcare resources.

Primary Education

In the education sector, Windo-L empowers the community by providing career guidance, mentoring youth, and sponsoring vulnerable individuals for tertiary education. We emphasize skill development, fostering entrepreneurship to combat unemployment. Our focus on education equality underscores the importance of knowledge and skills for community development, ensuring a brighter future for all

Youth Mentorship

Windo-L conducts Youth Mentoring/Life Skills programs in schools and outreach clinics. We provide essential life skills training, mentoring, and support to empower young individuals for a brighter future.
